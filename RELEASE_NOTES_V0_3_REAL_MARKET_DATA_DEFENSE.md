# v0.3-real-market-data-defense

`v0.3-real-market-data-defense` extends Financial Agent Evidence OS from deterministic benchmark evidence to sealed real-market ETF evidence.

This release does not claim live trading readiness, future return prediction, return guarantees, or market dominance.

The candidate strategy showed weak real-market performance, and that result is intentionally preserved as evidence. The product claim is that the Evidence OS can verify, scope, and seal financial AI performance claims on sealed ETF data.

Full provider-derived ETF CSV snapshots are not redistributed in the public repository because redistribution rights are unclear. The public repository includes the manifest, claim contract, schema sample, code, docs, and dashboard/Ralph tooling; full CSV snapshots remain local/private artifacts unless separately licensed.
